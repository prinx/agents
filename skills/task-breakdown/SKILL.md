# Task Breakdown

Group small ordered tickets into independently useful milestones, also called buckets. For every milestone, record a goal, included ticket IDs, acceptance criteria, dependencies or risks, and an expected review point. Give each ticket a stable ID, single outcome, acceptance criteria, dependencies, and state. Keep implementation and verification visible. Mark only the next executable ticket `Ready`; do not make a later milestone ready until the current milestone is complete and reviewed, and do not claim work is complete without evidence.
