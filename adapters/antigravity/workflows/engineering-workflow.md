# Engineering Workflow

Use the workflow-toolkit rule and relevant skill. Classify the request, create dynamic specialist subagents as necessary, keep artifact ownership explicit, relay planner discovery questions to the human, and stop until required answers or explicit approval arrive before developer handoff. At planning approval, record checkpointed or autonomous delivery. Check every milestone against requirements and confirmed decisions before starting it; complete and review it before the next milestone, with human review before and after milestones only in checkpointed mode.
